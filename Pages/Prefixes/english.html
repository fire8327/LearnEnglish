Prefix Description Examples
Un Not. Added to adjectives, verbs, adverbs. Unhappy, Unlearned, Unknown
Im Not. Added to adjectives that begin with b, m, p. Improbable, Immortal, Imbalance
Il Not. Added to adjectives that begin with l. Illegal, Illiterate
Ir Not. Added to adjectives that begin with r. Irregular, Irresponsible
In Not. Incorrect, Inconsistent
Dis Not or opposite. Added to adjectives, nouns, verbs. Disorder, Dishonest, Disobedient
Mis Badly or wrongly. Added to nouns and verbs. Misunderstand, Misbehave
Non Opposite. Non-alcoholic, Non-essential
Anti Against. Antitank, Antifascist
Counter Against. Counterbalance, Counter-revolution
Ex Former. Ex-champion
Pre Before. Predetermine, Prehistoric
Fore Front or forward. Forehead, Foreground
Post After. Postwar, Postmeridian
Sub Under. Submarine, Subway
Under Not enough. Underestimate, Underground
Inter Between. International
Trans Across or on the other side. Transatlantic, Transnational
Co With or together. Cooperate, Co-worker
Over Too much. Overpay, Oversleep
Super Higher or improved. Supernatural, Supermarket
Ultra Higher or extreme. Ultra-modern, Ultraviolet
En Action. Verbs from adjectives and nouns. Enlarge, Enrich
De Deprive. Verbs from nouns, often meaning "remove". Decode, Detrain
Re Again. Rewrite, Relive
